GCode Formatters¶

class hotwing_core.gcode_formatters.GcodeFormatBase[source]¶

Gcode Formatter’s job is to convert the commands maintained by the Gcode class and format them into the appropriate format for the specification of Gcode being generated.

On any subclass, you are expected to implement at a minimum the _process_move and _process_fast_move commands.
